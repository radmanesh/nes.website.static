Drone Operator in Houston, Texas

We are looking for an individual with experience flying drones who will be able to collect aerial photos and videos from construction sites. In addition to the imaging by drones the operator should be able to take regular videos and images from the site and be able to work with professional cameras (training will be provided).

The successful candidate can expect to travel to project sites in and around the Houston, TX to take picture and record videos from the drilling and construction process at sites based on the plan, tracks and waypoints provided by the supervisor.

Responsibilities

  • Operate a drone to take aerial photos in a designated flight pattern
  • Collect accurate measurements using a laser or tape measure
  • Communicate/interact with customers and clients
  • Coordinate with other site technicians and office staff
  • Upload data collected on site to shared Dropbox folder

Qualifications and Skills

  • Experience flying drones
  • Valid driver's license
  • Valid Part 107 FAA issued drone pilot license
  • Access to a vehicle
  • Access to a drone an asset is a plus
