Subsurface Mapping

Subsurface Utility Locating/Mapping

NES provides SUL/SUM services which help developers, excavators, drillers, engineers and other team members to have a better understanding of what they should expect to see at their sites.
In addition to marking the underground utilities or detectable buried elements at site, we prepare our deliverables in three different format listed below:

  • SUM-Basic Report
  • SUM-PRO Report
  • SUM-ULT Report

By utilizing Triple Bandwidth technology, we can also detect thickness of the pavement layers and demonestrate them in our drawings and reports. Ask our representative to learn more.
