Subsurface Mapping

The non-invasive subsurface mapping is performed by utilizing the most recent applications of geophysics to draw a 2D or 3D images of what is underlying an innocent surface.

Report Type SUM-Basic

  • The information regarding the scope of work and utilized methods.
  • Pictures of the marked locations at the site.

Report Type SUM-PRO

  • Includes all features of SUM-Basic.
  • Plan of the site and underground utilities.

Report Type SUM-ULT

  • Includes all features of SUM-PRO.
  • 3D model of the utilities over a Google Earth file.
  • Eyebird view of the marked existing utility locations at site by utilizing drone technology.
