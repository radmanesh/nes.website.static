Geotechnical Drilling and Sampling

Boring Layout

NES can locate and mark the boring locations by utilizing handheld GPS equipment such as GARMIN GPSMAP series.

Read More

Boring Elevations and Sampling Depths

The relative elevation at each boring location can be determined by utilizing a theodolite unit. As the minimum, the sampling depths will be measured from the existing ground surface at the time of our field activities and will be reported in our field logs.

Read More

Subsurface Exploration Procedures

We drill soil borings with our truck-mounted drilling equipment using continuous flight augers (solid stem or hollow stem) to either the termination depth of the borings or auger refusal depth whichever reach first. Soil samples will be collected at depths requested by the client.

Read More

Property Disturbance

After completion of drilling and field testing, we backfilled our boring with auger cuttings and distribute the remained auger cutting in vicinity of borings.

Read More

Non-Invasive Subsurface Mapping

Non-invasive subsurface mapping is utilizing the most recent applications of geophysics to draw a 2D or 3D images of what is underlying an innocent surface.
