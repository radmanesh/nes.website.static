Equipments

GPRover

We utilize GPRover which is built on the award-winning Triple Bandwidth technology. Triple bandwidth offers drastically more depth as well as higher resolution than any other imaging technology on the market. In this technique three different waves with frequencies of 1000, 500, and 250 megahertz are prospected to the underlying subgrade which will provide the upmost resolution and deeper decay depth in compression with single and dual signal GPRs. This technology provides 2-inch resolution within the maximum decay depth of waves which can assist tasks from estimating the thickness of the pavement within the upper couple of inches of subgrade to presence of a utility line at depth as deep as 15 feet.

RD8100® Cable, Pipe and RF Marker Locators

RD8100® cable locator is engineered to deliver high precision for damage prevention when locating buried cables and pipes. RD8100 Containing a unique arrangement of 5 custom-manufactured, precision-ground antennas, it allows you to choose the best method to locate specific pipes and cables.

With utility infrastructures becoming more complex, locate professionals require more powerful, flexible tools. Features such as Current Direction and iLOC™ on the cable locator combine with the versatile Tx Transmitter range to deliver high-precision locates even in tough conditions.
